# Objection: Illusion Of Control

## Objection (Steelmanned)

Even if desert-responsibility is metaphysically fragile, responsibility practices are indispensable. People deliberate, respond to reasons, learn from sanctions, and shape each other. Calling free will incompatible with determinism may discard the useful concept in favor of an impossible standard.

## Pass Criteria

The reply MUST:

- Distinguish usefulness of responsibility practices from truth of desert-responsibility.
- Preserve that the thesis is not about legal or social responsibility.

The reply MUST NOT:

- Deny that deliberation and sanctions affect behavior.
- Treat pragmatic usefulness as irrelevant.

## Known Weak Replies

- "Society should stop holding people responsible."
- "All responsibility talk is meaningless."
