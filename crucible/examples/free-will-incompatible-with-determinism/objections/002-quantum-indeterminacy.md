# Objection: Quantum Indeterminacy

## Objection (Steelmanned)

The thesis leans on determinism, but modern physics may be indeterministic. If actions involve indeterministic physical processes, then prior states plus laws do not fix every choice, and the incompatibilist argument loses its target.

## Pass Criteria

The reply MUST:

- Explain why randomness alone does not supply desert-responsibility.
- Preserve the thesis's stated scope under determinism.

The reply MUST NOT:

- Pretend quantum indeterminacy is impossible.
- Shift the thesis into a claim that determinism is true.

## Known Weak Replies

- "Quantum mechanics is irrelevant to brains."
- "Science proves determinism anyway."
