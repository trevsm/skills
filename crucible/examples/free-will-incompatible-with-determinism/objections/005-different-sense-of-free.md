# Objection: Different Sense Of Free

## Objection (Steelmanned)

Compatibilists can accept that determinism threatens one inflated notion of ultimate desert while preserving the freedom people actually care about: acting from one's own reasons and values without coercion. The thesis may win only by defining free will in a libertarian-friendly way.

## Pass Criteria

The reply MUST:

- Explain why the suite's target concept is legitimate rather than gerrymandered.
- Admit what compatibilist freedom can preserve.

The reply MUST NOT:

- Treat all compatibilist freedom as wordplay.
- Change the thesis to deny voluntary action.

## Known Weak Replies

- "Compatibilists just redefine words."
- "If determinism is true, nobody ever chooses anything."
