# Reply: Retraining Lift

## Reply

Training is part of the adoption cost, not an optional benefit. Rust becomes a default only after the pilots prove that at least two teams can build, review, deploy, and operate services without a single specialist bottleneck. The adoption plan must include paired implementation, review checklists, incident drills, and ownership rotation before Rust expands beyond pilots.

## Commitments This Reply Locks In
